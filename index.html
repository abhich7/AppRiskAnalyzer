FAQ

Common questions

Does Privacy Analyzer send my data anywhere?
No. Privacy Analyzer works entirely offline. Your scan results, app list, and permission data never leave your device. There are no servers, no analytics, and no accounts.
Do I need to root my phone?
Absolutely not. Privacy Analyzer uses Android's official PackageManager API which is available to all apps. No special permissions, no ADB, no root required.
Why is it an APK and not on the Play Store?
The app uses QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission which Google restricts on the Play Store. This permission is essential to scan all installed apps — without it, the app can only see a limited subset. Sideloading lets us provide full functionality.
Which Android versions are supported?
Android 10 (API 29) through Android 15 (API 35). The app adjusts its Settings navigation intents automatically based on your Android version.
Can Privacy Analyzer actually revoke permissions?
Android does not allow third-party apps to revoke permissions programmatically — that would itself be a security issue. Privacy Analyzer opens the exact Android Settings page for each permission so you can revoke it yourself in one tap.
